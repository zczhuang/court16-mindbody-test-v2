Court 16 × Cedarwind · Package A · Spec 08

Four email templates

Catalog of every email the new automation sends. Two are already drafted in the repo; two are placeholders for Ibtissam to draft.

What
Four email templates: staff notification, parent confirmation, password setup, 24-hour reminder. Plus the denial-reason variants the spec 05 workflow branches on.
Why
Everything the app does ends in an email. Writing the copy once, in one doc, is the difference between a clean kickoff and a week of tweaking.
Who
The two drafted templates were written with Ibtissam during Phase 1. The two greenfield ones + denial copy are Ibtissam's work with Cedarwind support at kickoff.
Status
Partial · 2 of 4 drafted
The two drafted templates below come verbatim from docs/hubspot-workflows.md in the repo. That file is the canonical source; this doc is its client-facing view.

Template 1 · Staff trial notification

Drafted in repo

Trigger & workflow

  • Workflow: Workflow 1 — Staff trial notification.
  • Current trigger: Contact property court16_booking_status equals pending_staff AND court16_intent equals kid_trial.
